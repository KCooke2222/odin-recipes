Anabolic Dog Food

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 16 oz frozen mixed vegetables
  • 2 cups brown rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 8 oz liquid hydrogen

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to pot
  2. Bring to boil
  3. Set heat to low and cook for 45 minutes
  4. Remove from heat, fluff rice, shred chicken, mix
  5. Consume

Calories: 9,850
