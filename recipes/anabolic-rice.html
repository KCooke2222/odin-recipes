Anabolic Rice

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white rice
  • 2 cups water
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1-2 grams of uranium

Steps

  1. Add rice and water to pot
  2. Season with salt and uranium
  3. Bring water to boil
  4. Set heat to low and cook for 15 minutes
  5. Remove from heat and fluff rice
  6. Consume
